Dominoes Engine Project

Github Reposository

Overview:

Dominoes is a very prevalent game in my culture, which inspired me to want to see if I could create an AI model to play 2 Player dominoes. In Puerto Rico, Dominoes is a very popular pastime. I have fond memories of playing with my Mother and Abuela. Despite being a simple game, there is lots of strategy and deduction involved higher level play, which spurred my interest in creating an AI player to try and develop stratagies.

Rules:

Dominoes is a popular game across the Carribean as well as in other Latin Countries, with many regional variations. I will quickly detail the rules I used, but if you want a more comprehensive overview, click here. At the beginning of the game each player draws 7 dominoes, the player with the highest value double goes first. If neither player has a double, the player with the most valueble domino goes first. Players then alternate placing dominoes on one of the two sides, playing a domino with a matching suit
